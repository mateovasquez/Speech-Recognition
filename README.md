# Speech Recognition
An app that recognizes your voice and shows what you are saying. One of the [30 Day Vanilla JS Challenge](https://github.com/wesbos/JavaScript30) by [Wes Bos](https://github.com/wesbos).
### Requisites:
You will need to allow your browser to access to your microphone and also activate it in your computer.
